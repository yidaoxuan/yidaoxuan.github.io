Dao Dao - Vacationer

Practice

Dao Dao designs buildings with the characteristics of a monster: unruly and romantic.
Conceptually rooted in the spirit of revolution in architecture, we seek to break down the status quo and bring a little bit of chaos to the built environment.
We work towards changing the perception of how architecture serves us and how we accept architecture.
We do not hope it is merely a friendly one.

We are remotely established in New York and Seoul.
We work at all scales.
We occasionally write to keep ourselves alert.
