Vacationer

Location N/A
Program Portable Shelter
Area 170, 120, or 70 SF
Structure Cross Laminated Timber
Exterior Finish Recycled Rubber

Vacationer is a portable shelter made of CLT panels with a thick layer of recycled rubber on the exterior. The rubber skin throughout the envelope works as a moisture and vapor barrier, while the shock-absorbent rubber mass at all eight corners helps the structure withstand physical impact. Due to its material conditions, it can travel to and settle down at unusual site conditions.

Vacationer provides different spaces depending on which plane becomes the grounding plane. When the orientation of the building changes, that of doors and windows will change accordingly, providing a completely different feel of interior living space.
